Golden Mule

Our premium twist on the classic Moscow Mule

Experience the refreshing blend of ginger and premium vodka in our signature copper mug

Taste Profile

The Golden Mule delivers a perfect balance of spicy ginger and smooth vodka. The initial taste is bright and effervescent from the ginger beer, followed by the crisp, clean finish of premium vodka. The fresh lime adds a tangy citrus note that complements the ginger's warmth. Served in a chilled copper mug, it maintains its refreshing coolness throughout your experience.

Golden Mule Description

₦3,200

A sophisticated take on the classic Moscow Mule, featuring premium vodka, spicy ginger beer, fresh lime juice, and a hint of mango syrup for a tropical twist. Served in our signature copper mug to enhance the flavors and maintain the perfect chill.

History & Origin

Created in 2023 by our lead mixologist Chinedu Okoro, the Golden Mule was inspired by traditional Russian cocktails but reimagined with African tropical flavors. Chinedu wanted to create a drink that honored the classic Moscow Mule while adding a distinctive Nigerian twist with premium local ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz premium vodka
  • 1/2 oz fresh lime juice
  • 4 oz premium ginger beer
  • 1/2 oz mango syrup
  • Fresh mint for garnish
  • Lime wheel for garnish

Preparation

  1. Fill a copper mug with crushed ice
  2. Add vodka and fresh lime juice
  3. Pour in ginger beer, leaving about 1/2 inch at the top
  4. Drizzle mango syrup over the top
  5. Gently stir to combine ingredients
  6. Garnish with fresh mint and a lime wheel
  7. Serve immediately with a straw

Taste Profile

The Golden Mule delivers a perfect balance of spicy ginger and smooth vodka. The initial taste is bright and effervescent from the ginger beer, followed by the crisp, clean finish of premium vodka. The fresh lime adds a tangy citrus note that complements the ginger's warmth. Served in a chilled copper mug, it maintains its refreshing coolness throughout your experience.

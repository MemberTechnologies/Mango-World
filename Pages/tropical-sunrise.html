Tropical Sunrise

Our signature cocktail that captures the essence of paradise

Experience the vibrant colors and tropical flavors of our signature cocktail

Taste Profile

The Tropical Sunrise offers a perfect balance of sweet and tangy flavors. The initial taste is bright and citrusy from the orange and pineapple, followed by the lush sweetness of mango. The vodka provides a clean, smooth finish that doesn't overpower the fruit flavors. As you drink, the layers gradually blend, creating an evolving flavor experience.

Tropical Sunrise Description

₦3,500

A visually stunning cocktail that layers mango puree, pineapple juice, and orange juice to create a beautiful sunrise effect. Topped with a splash of grenadine that slowly sinks to create a vibrant gradient reminiscent of a tropical sunrise.

History & Origin

Created in 2024 by our head mixologist, Emerald Oche, the Tropical Sunrise was inspired by the breathtaking sunrises he witnessed while moving through River Benue's bridge to the North Bank. Oche wanted to capture the vibrant colors and refreshing flavors of tropical mornings in a glass, creating what has become our most popular signature cocktail.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz premium vodka
  • 1 oz fresh mango puree
  • 2 oz pineapple juice
  • 2 oz fresh orange juice
  • 0.5 oz grenadine
  • Fresh mint for garnish
  • Orange slice for garnish

Preparation

  1. Fill a highball glass with ice
  2. Add mango puree and vodka, stir gently
  3. Slowly pour pineapple juice over the back of a spoon to layer
  4. Repeat with orange juice to create distinct layers
  5. Drizzle grenadine over the top and let it sink naturally
  6. Garnish with fresh mint and an orange slice
  7. Serve with a straw and enjoy the sunrise!

Taste Profile

The Tropical Sunrise offers a perfect balance of sweet and tangy flavors. The initial taste is bright and citrusy from the orange and pineapple, followed by the lush sweetness of mango. The vodka provides a clean, smooth finish that doesn't overpower the fruit flavors. As you drink, the layers gradually blend, creating an evolving flavor experience.
